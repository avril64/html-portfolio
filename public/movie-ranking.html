The Best Movies According to Avril

The top 3 movies of all-time.

Clueless

A classic feel good movie. 90's twist on Austen's Emma. The lines are hilarous. Even though I know every word, it still makes me laugh.

Being John Malkovich

Such an interesting concept. Philosophical and fanastical.

Requiem for a dream

Depressingly real. Beautifully shot. A stark slap in the face of how addiction infiltrates our lives in different forms and what happenns when we follow escapism down the rabbit hole.
